Data Collection

Trevor collected data on the superparamagnetic micro-robots by applying a magnetic field of varying strength in milliteslas. This involved going into the lab and using a playstation controller to move around the robot in a custom apparatus that applied magnetic fields to move the robot in the direction of the joystick. While moving the robot around a video is taken. Overall Trevor took 5 videos where the videos named with millitesla or mT is the robot moving right with a magnetic field of that amount applied to it. Then the last video named "Square" is the robot moving in a square motion.
